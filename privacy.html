Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective date: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains in plain language what information RAAV collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We do not sell your data. We do not show you ads. We collect only what is needed to make the app work.

1. Who we are

RAAV is a fitness tracking application developed and operated by Yonatan Slivko ("we", "us", or "our"), based in Israel. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at raavsupport@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account and use the app. This includes:

  • Account information: Email address, display name, profile photo (optional), and phone number (optional).
  • Body metrics: Height, current weight, biological sex, date of birth, estimated body fat percentage, lean body mass, and activity level — entered voluntarily by you to personalise your experience.
  • Health and fitness data: Workout logs, exercise records, set and repetition data, personal records, body weight history, and training program data you create or are assigned.
  • Goal and program data: Diet phase, calorie targets, program start and end dates, and training preferences you set.
  • Session data: Workout session timestamps and durations, exercise video uploads you submit (stored in Firebase Storage).

We do not collect data from your device's health platform (Apple Health, Google Fit) unless you explicitly connect them in a future update. We do not collect location data, contacts, microphone input, or camera input beyond profile photo upload.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect solely to provide, maintain and improve the RAAV application. Specifically:

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate you securely.
  • To store and display your workout history, personal records and progress over time.
  • To calculate and display fitness metrics such as estimated 5-rep maximums, training volume and progress percentages.
  • To enable program sharing between users where you explicitly choose to send or receive a program.
  • To respond to support requests you send to our contact email.

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing profiling, or sale to third parties. We do not use your data to train machine learning models.

4. Local storage and device data

The RAAV app stores certain data locally on your device to improve performance and enable offline functionality. This includes:

  • Authentication tokens: A secure token is stored locally on your device by Firebase Authentication to keep you signed in between sessions. This token does not contain your password and is invalidated when you sign out.
  • App preferences: Settings such as your selected unit of measurement (kg/cm or lbs/in) and display preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Cached data: Recent workout and exercise data may be temporarily cached locally to improve app load speed.

This locally stored data remains on your device and is cleared when you uninstall the app or sign out. We do not use cookies in the traditional web sense, as RAAV is a native mobile application.

5. How your information is stored and protected

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore database and Firebase Storage), a cloud infrastructure service operated by Google LLC. Firebase is hosted in Google's secure data centres and is compliant with ISO 27001, SOC 1, SOC 2 and SOC 3 security standards.

Access to your data is controlled by Firebase security rules. Only you can read and write your own data, with the limited exception that training programs you choose to send to other users make your program name and workout structure visible to the recipient.

Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication using industry-standard encryption. We never store passwords in plaintext.

While we implement strong technical safeguards, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

6. Data breach notification

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and relevant supervisory authorities as required by applicable law. Specifically:

  • We will notify affected users by email within 72 hours of becoming aware of a qualifying breach, where notification is technically feasible.
  • The notification will describe the nature of the breach, the categories of data affected, the likely consequences, and the steps we are taking to address it.
  • We maintain an internal log of all data security incidents, whether or not they qualify as notifiable breaches, as part of our ongoing security practices.

If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at raavsupport@gmail.com.

7. Sharing of your information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties.

The only third-party service that processes your data is Google Firebase, which acts as our infrastructure provider under their terms of service and privacy policies. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

8. Data retention

We retain your personal data and fitness records for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information and fitness data from our active databases within 30 days. Residual copies in backup systems are purged within 90 days.

Exercise videos you upload are deleted when you delete the associated exercise or delete your account.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all personal data and fitness records stored in your account directly within the app at any time.
  • Correction: You can edit your personal information, body metrics and goals within the app's settings at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app settings, or by contacting us at raavsupport@gmail.com.
  • Data portability: You may request an export of your data by contacting us. We will provide it in a readable format within 30 days.
  • Objection: You may object to any processing of your data at any time by deleting your account or contacting us.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Israel, you have rights under the GDPR or applicable local data protection law. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at raavsupport@gmail.com.

10. Children's privacy

RAAV is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at raavsupport@gmail.com and we will delete that information promptly.

Users between the ages of 13 and 17 should use the app with parental consent.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable law. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app.

Your continued use of RAAV after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

12. Governing law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Israel. Any disputes relating to this policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Israel.
